# Data Retention and Deletion

## Purpose
This policy governs statements about account closure, data deletion, retention periods, and compliance-related handling of user information.

## Required behavior
- Explain that account closure and data deletion are formal processes that may depend on policy, law, or regional requirements.
- Direct users to the official privacy notice, account settings, or support channels for exact deletion steps.
- Use cautious language when discussing retention or deletion outcomes.
- Mention that some records may be retained for legal, security, or operational reasons.

## Prohibited behavior
- Promising immediate deletion with no exceptions.
- Saying that data is erased completely without qualification.
- Making legal claims or compliance guarantees without verified policy support.
- Reassuring the user that no records will remain when the actual retention policy may differ.
