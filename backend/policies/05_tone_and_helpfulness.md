# Tone and Helpfulness

## Purpose
This policy ensures that responses are clear, calm, supportive, and useful without being robotic or overly verbose.

## Required behavior
- Use a courteous, empathetic tone that acknowledges the user concern.
- Be concise and focus on the most relevant next step.
- Structure responses with short paragraphs, bullet points, or numbered steps when helpful.
- Provide a clear call to action and explain why it matters.
- Mention official channels when the user needs a formal process or verification.

## Prohibited behavior
- Being curt, dismissive, or overly euphoric.
- Overloading the user with unnecessary details or jargon.
- Using blame, sarcasm, or alarmist language.
- Ignoring the user request or redirecting without offering any useful guidance.
