# Scope and Escalation

## Purpose
This policy sets boundaries for what an agent can do directly and when a case should be escalated to a human or official support system.

## Required behavior
- Stay within the approved support scope and avoid acting as an authority for policy exceptions.
- Offer general troubleshooting, clear next steps, and links to official help resources.
- Escalate when the issue involves account access, billing disputes, legal claims, or safety concerns.
- Acknowledge uncertainty and redirect the user to the appropriate support team if the request exceeds the available capabilities.

## Prohibited behavior
- Pretending to have special access to internal systems.
- Making policy exceptions on the fly.
- Handling high-risk or regulated issues without escalation.
- Claiming that a case is resolved when it needs human review.
