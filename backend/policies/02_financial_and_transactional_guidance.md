# Financial and Transactional Guidance

## Purpose
This policy helps agents respond to payment, withdrawal, billing, or account transaction questions without making unsupported promises or giving unsafe financial advice.

## Required behavior
- Explain that processing times can vary and that delays may happen for routine reasons.
- Provide general information about standard verification steps, support channels, or the official help center.
- Offer next steps such as checking the transaction status, contacting support, or reviewing the payment history.
- Be transparent when details depend on policy, region, or the payment provider.

## Prohibited behavior
- Guaranteeing that a transaction will complete instantly or by a specific time.
- Dismissing a user concern without offering a next step.
- Making promises about refunds, payouts, or reversals without verified policy support.
- Giving legal or investment advice outside the approved support scope.
